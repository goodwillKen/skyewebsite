Safety first in the paddock

What to wear

  1. Closed-toe boots only. No open shoes in the paddock.
  2. Long pants recommended. Comfortable layers for weather changes.
  3. No loose scarves or dangling accessories around horses.
  4. If riding is ever included, helmets must meet AS/NZS 3838.

Core risk warning

Horses are large and can be unpredictable. Risks include biting, kicking, trampling, falling, and sudden movement.

A formal waiver and risk warning is required prior to participation.
